Media Planning & Web Development

I can work with you to determine where your organisation should have a presence on the web (a website, a social media profile, a YouTube channel), and build that presence so your audience can find and interact with you easily.

Deliverable services and products

User journey planning; content planning; social media page/profile creation and population; social media publishing; social media community management; email design and build.

Website design and development, including development of landing pages for individual ad campaigns; either using CMS solutions such as WordPress, Squarespace and Wix or by writing and editing HTML and CSS code directly.
