Analytics & User Tracking

Predominantly using Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics, I implement digital user tracking and analytics solutions for a number of clients.

That means a client can understand who their website’s users are, how those users arrived at the website, whether they’re using the site in a valuable way and how their website can be changed to improve engagement.

Deliverable services and products

Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics implementations, audits and modifications; Enhanced Ecommerce implementations; advanced content engagement tracking; Google Analytics custom dimension and metric implementations.
